About Mantrabhay

Mantrabhay is a devotional creative project dedicated to the timeless spiritual traditions of Sanatan Dharma.

Through carefully crafted video presentations and mantra recitations, Mantrabhay makes sacred devotional literature accessible to modern seekers worldwide.

The platform features revered compositions including Bhairavastava, Shiv Tandav Stotram, Kal Bhairav Ashtakam, Ravan Rachit Shiv Dhyanam, Ramashtakam, and Shri Ram Tandav Stotram.
