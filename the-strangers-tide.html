The Stranger’s Tide

A Story of Love, Discipline, and Identity Reconstruction

Written by Abhay Kumar, founder of Mantrabhay, "The Stranger’s Tide" is a reflective narrative exploring love, inner discipline, and the reconstruction of identity through life’s profound experiences. Blending emotional depth with philosophical introspection, the book invites readers to journey through the silent battles of the heart, the search for meaning, and the power of personal transformation.
