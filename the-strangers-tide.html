About the Book

The Stranger’s Tide is a reflective narrative exploring the quiet transformation that occurs when love, discipline, and solitude intersect in the human experience.

Through philosophical introspection and emotional storytelling, the book examines how identity evolves through life's silent struggles, personal sacrifices, and moments of deep reflection.

Blending emotional depth with spiritual undertones, the narrative invites readers to explore the internal tides that shape character, purpose, and personal truth.

Key Themes

• Love and emotional depth
• Discipline and inner transformation
• Identity reconstruction
• Silence and introspection
• Spiritual philosophy in modern life

“Sometimes the deepest transformations begin in silence, where no one sees the tide turning.”

About the Author

Abhay Kumar is the founder of Mantrabhay, a devotional music and spiritual poetry project dedicated to Sanskrit mantras, sacred chants, and spiritual literature inspired by the traditions of Sanatan Dharma.

Alongside his creative work, he is a Senior Financial Specialist in Commercial Underwriting at American Express.

The Stranger’s Tide marks his debut literary work exploring the themes of love, discipline, and personal transformation.
