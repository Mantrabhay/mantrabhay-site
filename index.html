About Mantrabhay

Mantrabhay is a devotional music project dedicated to Sanskrit stotras, sacred mantras and spiritual poetry inspired by Lord Shiva. The platform presents powerful compositions such as Bhairavastava, Shiv Tandav Stotram and Ravan Rachit Shiv Dhyanam.

These works combine traditional Sanskrit chanting with atmospheric devotional music to create immersive spiritual experiences. The Mantrabhay project explores ancient Hindu scripture, meditation music and poetic devotion through modern digital platforms including YouTube, Spotify and social media.
